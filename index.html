Kobe's Car Experiences

This is a article about the past can current cars that i've had, since I started driving.

My First Car

My very first car, at the age of 17. Was a 2004 Buick Regal LS.

This vehicle came with the 3.8L V6 motore paired with a 4 speed automatic transmission.

I bought it with about 107k miles on the dash for only $3000.

Although, it had a good about of mileage on the dash. This car never gave me any issues and drove great!

I had this vehicle, up to about 111k miles, before trading it in for my next vehicle.

My Second Car

My second vehicle was a 2021 Kia Forte GT-Line
I bought this vehicle brand new, with only 21 miles on the dash.

It came with a 2.0L inline 4 motor, paired with a CVT transmission

I absolutely loved this car! I gave it some modifications including >

Tinted Windows, Headlights, and Tail lights
Front Splitter
Rear Canards
Carbon Fiber Ducktail Wing
Underglow
Aftermarket Wheels and Tires

I drove that until I traded it in at about 32k miles

My Current Car

My current vehicle is a 2023 Kia Forte GT

It has a 1.6L Turbo motor, with a 7 speed Dual Clutch transmision paired with it

I love this car even more than the last! Though, I haven't done any modifications to it yet. But soon!!
